📖 About FWI Index

The Fire Weather Index (FWI) is a standardized system used by meteorologists and foresters to assess fire danger. It combines weather conditions with fuel moisture codes to provide a comprehensive measurement of forest fire danger. Values range from 0-100+, with higher values indicating increased fire danger and risk.
